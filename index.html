About Us

Who is Summit To Share?

We are a group of mates from Devon, on a bit of a mission. We are getting together to push for the greater connection of young people to the outdoors, starting with the beautiful landscapes of our home county.

Why was Summit To Share Started?

We believe that getting out and roaming our countryside is one of the greatest tools for tackling issues around mental health. And we want to celebrate that by getting as many people on board with that belief as possible, sharing their stories with one another in the process.
