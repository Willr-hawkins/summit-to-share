About Us

Who is Summit To Share?

Summit To Share was started by a group of mates that share the same passion for the outdoors.

Why was Summit To Share Started?

We started Summit To Share to improve awareness around the relationship between hiking outdoors and mental health.

What is our goal?

Our goal is to encourage more people to go outside with friends and family, and to prove to people it is important to talk about their feelings to one another.
