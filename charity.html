Who is Ask For Jake?

Our chosen Charity

Ask For Jake is a non-profit charity that provides a first point of contact for people that are struggling with their mental health. They do not only focus on suicide but also, self-harming, eating and behaviour issues, drug and alcohol dependancy, and any other issues.

Where are they based?

Ask For Jake is located in Braunton North Devon, and was started up due to shocked death of a young 18 year old from the Braunton community.

Why we chose Ask For Jake?

We chose to work alongside Ask For Jake as we all live in the North Devon area and wanted to support a local charity. And throughout our first group fundrasier (Project: Hike for Max) they supported us and helped us get our name out there to hopefully reach more people.
